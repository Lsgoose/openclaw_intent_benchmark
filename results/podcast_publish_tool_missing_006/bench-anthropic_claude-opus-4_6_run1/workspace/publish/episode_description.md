# Episode 14: Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

How does a raw recording become a polished, discoverable episode? In this conversation, host and guest Maya Chen break down the five stages of a podcast production pipeline—capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution—and explain why production discipline matters even for small teams.

They discuss where handoffs break down, why a raw transcript is not the same as publish-ready content, and how to design a lightweight system that keeps quality consistent without slowing you down. Maya shares practical advice on title strategy, pre-publish checks, and the difference between automating drafts versus automating approvals.

Whether you produce solo or with a small crew, this episode offers a clear framework for turning messy recordings into reliable releases every single time.
