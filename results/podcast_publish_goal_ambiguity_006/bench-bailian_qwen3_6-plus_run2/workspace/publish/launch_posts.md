# Launch Posts

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

The most important lesson from this episode: a transcript is a rich input, not the final source of editorial truth. Use automation to accelerate drafts, but keep a human in the loop for anything that goes public. Consistency beats complexity every time.

## Post 2 — Discussion Question

What's the one handoff in your content workflow that causes the most friction? If your recording, editing, and publishing steps don't share a single source of truth, publishing quality becomes luck. What would you fix first?
