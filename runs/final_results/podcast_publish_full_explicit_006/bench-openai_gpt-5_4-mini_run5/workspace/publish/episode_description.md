Episode 14 examines how to build a reliable podcast production pipeline from capture to distribution. Maya Chen explains why the pipeline is the product, how small teams avoid handoff chaos, and why raw transcripts should never be treated as final truth. The conversation covers clean vs. raw transcripts, repeatable output bundles, title strategy, metadata consistency, and the checks that matter before publish. The episode closes with a practical framework for using automation to accelerate drafts without surrendering editorial judgment.
