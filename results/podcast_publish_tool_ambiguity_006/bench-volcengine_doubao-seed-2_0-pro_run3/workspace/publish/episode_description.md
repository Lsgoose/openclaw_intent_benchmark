Episode 14: Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

Ever wondered how a raw recording becomes a polished podcast episode complete with clean audio, chapters, show notes, and social assets? Media production advisor Maya Chen breaks down the 5 core pipeline stages every show needs, common pain points for small teams, and how to balance automation with editorial judgment.

Learn how to design a lightweight, reliable pipeline that avoids version chaos, improves listener experience, and reduces last-minute mistakes. Perfect for small creator teams looking to scale their production without sacrificing quality.
