**Guest:** Maya Chen  
**Published:** 2026-04-19

## Episode Description

What separates a professional podcast from an amateur one often isn't the recording itself—it's the pipeline that gets it from raw audio to published episode.

In Episode 14, Maya Chen (media systems advisor) joins us to break down the five stages of a reliable podcast production pipeline: capture, post-production, content extraction, review and approval, and distribution. We discuss why small teams struggle with handoffs, how to separate raw from cleaned transcripts, and where automation helps versus where it fails.

**Key topics:**
- The five stages every pipeline needs
- Why "final_v2_real_final.wav" is a warning sign
- Editorial judgment that machines can't make
- How to build a lightweight but consistent workflow
