# Episode Description

Maya Chen, who advises media teams and creator businesses on production systems, joins the show to unpack how podcast production pipelines shape the entire listener experience. The conversation explores the five stages of production—from capture through distribution—and why small teams most often struggle with handoffs, inconsistent naming, and treating raw transcripts as publish-ready content. Maya makes a critical distinction between raw and clean transcripts, explains where automation helps versus where it fails, and offers a practical framework for generating titles that actually serve your audience. The episode includes a five-point pre-publish checklist and specific steps any small team can implement this month to build consistency without elaborate systems.
