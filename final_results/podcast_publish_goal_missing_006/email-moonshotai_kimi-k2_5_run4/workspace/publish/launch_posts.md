# Launch Posts — Episode 14: The Podcast Production Pipeline

## Key Takeaway

The transcript is a rich input, not the final source of editorial truth. Automate the repetitive transforms—cleanup, first drafts, formatting—but keep a human in the loop for anything that goes live. The pipeline isn't overhead; it's the product.

## Discussion Question

Where does your current production workflow depend on memory and heroics instead of clear handoffs—and what's the first checklist you'd write to fix it?
