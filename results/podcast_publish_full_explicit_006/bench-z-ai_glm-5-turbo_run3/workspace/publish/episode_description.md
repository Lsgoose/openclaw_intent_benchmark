# Episode 14: Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

Maya Chen joins the show to break down the five stages every podcast needs — capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution — and explain why the pipeline itself is the product. She covers where small teams stumble first (handoffs, file naming, treating raw transcripts as finished content), when to automate versus when a human must still review, and how to build a lightweight but consistent output bundle that makes every downstream task easier. The conversation also digs into title strategy, pre-publish checklists, and the mindset shift from heroics to repeatable systems. Whether you run a two-person show or a growing media operation, this episode offers a concrete roadmap to tighten your production without overcomplicating it.
