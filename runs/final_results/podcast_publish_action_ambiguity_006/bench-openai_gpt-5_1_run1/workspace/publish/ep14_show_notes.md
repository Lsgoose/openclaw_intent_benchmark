# Episode 14  Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

**Episode title:** How Small Teams Build a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

**Episode summary (short):**
A practical conversation with production systems advisor Maya Chen about how small podcast teams can turn a messy, memory-driven process into a reliable, repeatable production pipeline  from raw recording to polished, published episode.

**Episode summary (long):**
In this episode, we go behind the scenes of podcast production with consultant and media systems advisor **Maya Chen**. Together we break down the podcast production pipeline into five key stages: capture, post-production, content extraction, review and approval, and distribution. We talk about why the pipeline is effectively part of the product, why small teams struggle with handoffs and version chaos ("final_v2_real_final.wav"), and how to design a simple but robust workflow even if you only have two or three people.

Maya explains why raw transcripts are rich inputs but not publish-ready truth, and why its worth maintaining both a raw transcript for traceability and a cleaned transcript for readability and repurposing. We discuss where automation helps (drafting titles, summaries, chapters, and social copy) and where humans still need to own judgment, especially around facts, links, sponsor language, and sensitive edits.

If youre trying to make your show more consistent without turning it into a full-time operations job, this episode will give you a concrete model, a standard output bundle to aim for, and a set of low-friction practices you can start using this month.

**Key topics:**
- Why your production pipeline is part of the listener experience
- The five stages of a podcast production pipeline
- Common failure points in small-team workflows
- Raw vs. cleaned transcripts and why both matter
- How to use automation for acceleration, not abdication
- Generating and choosing effective episode titles
- High-value review checks before you publish
- Designing a standard output bundle and episode log
