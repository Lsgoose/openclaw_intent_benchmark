# Episode 14: The Podcast Production Pipeline — Episode Description

How does a raw recording become a polished, published episode? In this conversation, production systems advisor Maya Chen breaks down the five stages every podcast moves through — capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution — and explains why treating the pipeline as the product changes everything.

Maya and the host explore where small teams struggle first (handoffs, naming chaos, and mistaking transcripts for finished content), how to build a lightweight but reliable workflow, and where automation helps versus where human judgment remains essential. Maya shares her framework for generating better episode titles, outlines the five highest-value pre-publish checks, and offers a practical five-step plan any small show can implement this month.

Key takeaway: automate drafts, not approvals. Let machines produce options; let humans own what goes public.
