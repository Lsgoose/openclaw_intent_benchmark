# Episode 14 — Episode Description

Most podcasters think the product is the conversation. Production systems advisor Maya Chen argues it's actually the pipeline.

In this episode, Maya walks through the five stages of a reliable podcast production pipeline — capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution — and explains where small teams most often break down. The conversation covers the difference between raw and clean transcripts, why treating a transcript as publish-ready copy leads to low-quality derivative content, and how to design a lightweight but consistent output bundle that every episode ships with.

Maya also addresses the growing use of automation in podcast workflows, drawing a clear line between using tools to accelerate repetitive tasks and mistakenly delegating editorial judgment. The episode closes with a five-step action list any small team can implement immediately.

If your show has ever shipped with a wrong link, a mismatched title and description, or a version-confusion crisis in the edit folder, this episode is for you.
