Ever wondered how a raw recording becomes a polished podcast episode? This episode breaks down the end-to-end production pipeline that shapes every part of the listener experience, from clean audio to discoverable metadata. Media operations expert Maya Chen shares actionable frameworks for small teams, including core pipeline stages, common failure points, automation best practices, and pre-publish checklists to avoid costly mistakes. Whether you're a solo creator or part of a small team, learn how to build consistent, reliable systems that turn great conversations into great episodes without burnout.
