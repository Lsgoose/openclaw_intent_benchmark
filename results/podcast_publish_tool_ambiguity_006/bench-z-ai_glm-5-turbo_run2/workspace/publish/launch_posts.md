# Launch Posts — Episode 14

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

Automate drafts, not approvals. A good podcast pipeline accelerates repetitive transforms — transcription cleanup, first-draft notes, chapter suggestions — but keeps a human responsible for what goes live.
