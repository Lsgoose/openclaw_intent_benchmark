# Launch Posts — Episode 14

## Post 1: Key Takeaway

A transcript is a rich input, not the final source of editorial truth. Use automation to accelerate drafts — but never abdicate responsibility for what goes live. That's the difference between a pipeline and a mess.

## Post 2: Discussion Question

What's the first thing that breaks in your podcast workflow when you skip a step? For most teams it's metadata consistency — title says one thing, description says another. Where does your process crack first?
