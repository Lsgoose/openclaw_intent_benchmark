# Episode 14 — Metadata

**Title:** Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

**Short Description (≤ 160 characters):**
How small teams build consistent shows — from raw recording to polished release, with Maya Chen.

**Long Description:**
What separates a consistent podcast from a chaotic one? In Episode 14, media systems advisor Maya Chen breaks down the five-stage production pipeline — capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution — and explains why the pipeline itself is the product. Topics include transcript discipline, output bundle standardization, title strategy, automation boundaries, and the five highest-value pre-publish checks. Practical and immediately applicable for teams of any size.
