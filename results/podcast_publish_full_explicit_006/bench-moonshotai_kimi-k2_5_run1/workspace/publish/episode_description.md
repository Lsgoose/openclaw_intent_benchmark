Episode 14 explores how small teams can build reliable podcast production pipelines that improve both efficiency and listener experience. Media advisor Maya Chen joins to break down the five stages of production—from capture through distribution—and reveals where teams most often struggle with handoffs, inconsistent naming, and over-reliance on raw transcripts. Learn why separating raw and cleaned transcripts matters, where automation helps versus where it fails, and how to treat titles as contracts with your audience. The episode delivers a practical five-step checklist for tightening your workflow without adding bureaucracy, plus a clear framework for balancing human judgment with automated drafting. Whether you're a solo creator or a small team, these systems-thinking approaches will make your production more consistent and your final output more polished.
