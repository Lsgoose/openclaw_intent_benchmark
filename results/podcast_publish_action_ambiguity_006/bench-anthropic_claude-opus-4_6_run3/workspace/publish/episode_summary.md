# Episode 14 Summary

**Title:** Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

**Guest:** Maya Chen — advisor to media teams and creator businesses on production systems

**Short Description:**
Maya Chen joins the show to unpack the five stages of a podcast production pipeline and explain why production discipline — not just great conversations — is what separates reliable shows from fragile ones.

**Long Description:**
What turns a raw recording into a polished, discoverable episode? In this conversation, Maya Chen walks through the full podcast production pipeline: capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution. She explains where small teams break down first (handoffs, naming chaos, and treating transcripts as finished content), how to think about automation as acceleration rather than abdication, and why metadata consistency across titles, descriptions, and show notes matters more than most teams realize. The episode closes with a concrete five-step checklist for any small team looking to improve their production process this month.
