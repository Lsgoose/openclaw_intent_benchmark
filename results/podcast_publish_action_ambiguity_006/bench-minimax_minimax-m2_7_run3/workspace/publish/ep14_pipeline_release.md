## Core Themes (from transcript)
- The pipeline is the product — production discipline shapes the listener experience
- Five stages: capture → post-production → content extraction → review & approval → distribution
- Three common pain points: broken handoffs, inconsistent naming, treating raw transcript as publish-ready
- Raw transcript ≠ clean transcript — separate them
- Automation accelerates drafts; humans own approvals
- Titles are a contract with the listener, not just a label
- Five pre-publish checks: names, links, sponsor compliance, sensitive edits, metadata consistency

2. Create a publish checklist owned by one person
3. Separate raw transcript and cleaned transcript
4. Define the required output bundle for every episode
5. Keep a lightweight episode log to spot delays and repeated errors
