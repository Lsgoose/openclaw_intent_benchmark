# Episode 14 Launch Posts

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

Automating a podcast pipeline works best when it accelerates drafts, not approvals. Transcription cleanup, first-draft notes, chapter suggestions — great candidates. What goes live still needs a human in the seat. New episode on building reliable production systems is out now.

## Post 2 — Discussion Question

When a podcast episode feels inconsistent — the title promises one thing, the description covers something slightly different — do you notice it immediately, or only after you're already a few minutes in? Curious how much metadata alignment actually shapes first impressions.
