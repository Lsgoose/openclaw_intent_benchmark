## Post 1 – Launch announcement

New episode is live: **Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline for Small Teams**.

Consultant **Maya Chen** joins us to break down a five‑stage pipeline for taking an episode from raw recording to polished release:
- Capture
- Post‑production
- Content extraction
- Review & approval

## Post 2 – Transcripts & automation angle

Many shows treat the transcript as if it were final editorial truth.

In Episode 14, consultant **Maya Chen** explains why a good pipeline keeps **both** raw and cleaned transcripts—and treats them as inputs for summaries, chapters, and social copy, not as publish‑ready text.

We dig into where automation really helps (drafts, structure, packaging) and where humans still need to make the final call before anything goes live.

If you’re experimenting with agents or AI in your production flow, this one’s worth a listen.
