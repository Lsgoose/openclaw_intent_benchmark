# Episode 14 — Release Notes

**Title:** Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline
**Guest:** Maya Chen
**Sponsor:** SoundLayer

## Summary

Maya Chen breaks down the five-stage podcast production pipeline — capture, post-production, content extraction, review & approval, and distribution — and explains why production discipline matters as much as the conversation itself.

Key takeaways:
- A transcript is a rich input, not the final source of editorial truth
- Small teams benefit from standardized folder structures, publish checklists, and defined output bundles
- Automate drafts, not approvals
- Generate multiple title candidates from different angles and compare against audience intent
