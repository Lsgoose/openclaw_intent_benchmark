**Key takeaway**
A reliable podcast doesn’t just come from great conversations—it comes from a dependable production pipeline. Standardize your episode folder structure, keep both raw and cleaned transcripts, and treat the transcript as rich input (not final truth) so every episode ships with consistent, trustworthy assets.
