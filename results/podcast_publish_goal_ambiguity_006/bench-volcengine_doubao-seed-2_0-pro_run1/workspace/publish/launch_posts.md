# Launch Post 1 (Key Takeaway)
Use automation to accelerate repetitive podcast production tasks like transcript cleanup, first-draft notes, and metadata prefill — but always keep a human in the loop for final approval of public-facing content. Editorial judgment and edge case handling can't be fully automated yet.

# Launch Post 2 (Discussion Question)
What's the most common point of failure in your podcast production pipeline? Is it handoffs between team members, version confusion with assets, inconsistent metadata, or something else entirely?
