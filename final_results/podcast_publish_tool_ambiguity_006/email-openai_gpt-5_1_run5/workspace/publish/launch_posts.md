**Post 1 – Key takeaway**
A reliable podcast isn’t just great conversation and clean audio—it’s a production pipeline that treats transcripts as rich input, not final truth, standardizes outputs for every episode, and uses automation to accelerate drafts while humans still own what actually goes live.
