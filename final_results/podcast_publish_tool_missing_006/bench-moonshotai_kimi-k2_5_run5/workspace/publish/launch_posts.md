# Launch Posts

## Post 1: Key Takeaway

Your transcript is a rich input—not the final source of editorial truth. Raw transcripts carry filler, false starts, and unverified references that can mislead your downstream content. The teams that get this right maintain two versions: raw for traceability, cleaned for publishing. It's a small discipline that prevents awkward summaries, broken quote cards, and misleading snippets. The pipeline is the product.
