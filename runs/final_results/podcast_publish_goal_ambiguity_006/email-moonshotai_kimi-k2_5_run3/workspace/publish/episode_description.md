# Episode Description

Maya Chen joins the show to unpack what actually happens between hitting record and publishing a finished podcast episode. We walk through the five-stage production pipeline—capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution—and explore why small teams struggle with handoffs, inconsistent naming, and treating raw transcripts as publish-ready truth. Maya shares practical frameworks for lightweight systems, explains where automation helps and where it fails, and offers a repeatable strategy for titling that treats the headline as a contract with the listener. Whether you run a two-person show or are refining an existing workflow, this episode delivers a clear checklist for building a reliable, reviewable, and scalable production process.
