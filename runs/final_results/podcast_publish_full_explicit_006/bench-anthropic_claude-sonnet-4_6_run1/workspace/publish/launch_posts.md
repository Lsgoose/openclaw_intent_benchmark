# Episode 14 Launch Posts

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

A raw transcript is not publish-ready content. It contains filler, false starts, and unverified links. Treat it as a rich input, not the final source of editorial truth. Ep 14 breaks down how to build a production pipeline that catches these gaps before they go live.

## Post 2 — Discussion Question

When does automation help a podcast production workflow — and when does it create more problems than it solves? Ep 14 digs into where human judgment is still irreplaceable, even as more teams add AI tools to their pipeline.
