# Launch Posts

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

A raw transcript is a rich input, not the final source of editorial truth. The best podcast pipelines separate raw and cleaned transcripts, automate first drafts, and keep humans in charge of what goes live. Consistency beats complexity every time.
