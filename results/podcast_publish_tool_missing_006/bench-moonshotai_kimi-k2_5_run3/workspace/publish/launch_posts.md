# Launch Posts for Episode 14

## Post 1: Key Takeaway

Your podcast pipeline isn't invisible infrastructure—it's the product your listeners actually experience. Raw transcripts aren't publish-ready truth, and the best titles come from understanding what your episode helps listeners do, not the most repeated phrase in the conversation. Small teams don't need elaborate systems, just consistency: clean naming, defined outputs, and humans owning what goes live.
