# Launch Posts

## Post 1: Key Takeaway

The best production pipeline isn't the most automated — it's the most consistent. Small teams fail on handoffs without clear ownership and files named "final_v2_real_final.wav". Start with folder structure, naming conventions, and a single-owner checklist.

---

## Post 2: Discussion Question

Where does your production pipeline actually break? Not where you assume — where it creates real delays. Handoffs? Transcript cleanup? Title decisions? The final check? What's one checkpoint you'd add if you could only add one?
