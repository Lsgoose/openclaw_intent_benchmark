# Launch Post 1 (Key Takeaway)
A podcast production pipeline isn't just back-office ops—it's core to your listener experience. Small teams can avoid 90% of common release mistakes by standardizing one folder per episode, separating raw vs cleaned transcripts, and using automation for drafts, not final approvals.
