# Episode 14: Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

Maya Chen joins us to demystify how podcasts go from raw recording to polished, discoverable episodes. We walk through the five stages every production pipeline needs—capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution—and identify where small teams typically struggle: handoffs, inconsistent file naming, and assuming transcripts are publish-ready. Maya explains why automation should accelerate, not replace, human judgment, and why titles are a contract with your listener, not just labels. You'll come away with a practical five-step checklist to tighten your own workflow this month, plus a clear distinction between raw and cleaned transcripts that could save your downstream content from inheriting every messy conversation artifact.
