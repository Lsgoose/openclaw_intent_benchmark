This episode explores the podcast production pipeline as a system that shapes the final listener experience. Maya Chen walks through the full workflow from capture and post-production to content extraction, review, and distribution. The conversation highlights why clean transcripts, consistent naming, fixed output bundles, and strong review habits matter for small teams. It also covers the limits of automation, especially when editorial judgment, edge cases, sponsor compliance, and metadata consistency are involved. The core message is simple: use automation to accelerate drafts and repetitive transforms, but keep humans responsible for what goes live.
