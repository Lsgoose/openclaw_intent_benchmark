Episode 14 explores how to build a reliable podcast production pipeline from recording to release. Maya Chen breaks the workflow into capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution, then shows where small teams usually break down: handoffs, naming, transcript cleanup, and inconsistent approvals. The conversation emphasizes keeping raw and cleaned transcripts separate, using automation for drafts and repetitive transforms, and preserving human ownership for judgment, accuracy, and release quality. It also covers practical title strategy, the most important pre-publish checks, and a lightweight system that helps teams ship consistent episodes without overengineering the process.
