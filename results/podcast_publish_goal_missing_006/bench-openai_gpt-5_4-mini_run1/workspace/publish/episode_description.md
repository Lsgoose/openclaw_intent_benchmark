This episode explores the podcast production pipeline as the real product behind a polished show. Maya Chen walks through the five core stages: capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution. The conversation covers why clean transcripts matter, how small teams can reduce handoff failures, and where automation helps most without replacing editorial judgment. It also digs into title strategy, metadata consistency, and the checks that prevent sloppy releases. A practical episode for anyone building a repeatable publishing workflow.
