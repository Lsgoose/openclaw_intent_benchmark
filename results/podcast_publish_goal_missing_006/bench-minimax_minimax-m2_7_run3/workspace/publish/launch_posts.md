# Launch Posts — Episode 14: The Podcast Production Pipeline

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

A transcript is a rich input, not editorial truth. Raw transcripts contain filler, false starts, and unverified claims — treat them as draft material. A clean pipeline separates raw from cleaned before handing off to automation or teammates.

## Post 2 — Discussion Question

When a title promises one thing and the episode summary describes something different, listeners notice immediately. What systems does your show use to keep title, description, transcript, and chapter markers consistent before release — and who owns that check?
