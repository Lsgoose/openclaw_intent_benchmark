Episode 14 explores how reliable podcast production pipelines shape the listener experience. Maya Chen joins to break the workflow into five stages—from capture and post-production through content extraction, review, and distribution. The conversation covers why raw transcripts aren't publish-ready, how to automate drafts without abdicating judgment, and why a title is a contract with your audience. For small teams, Maya recommends starting with folder structure, a single-owner checklist, and a defined output bundle for every episode. Whether you're running a two-person show or scaling a creator business, this episode offers practical systems for cleaner handoffs, consistent metadata, and fewer pre-launch surprises.
