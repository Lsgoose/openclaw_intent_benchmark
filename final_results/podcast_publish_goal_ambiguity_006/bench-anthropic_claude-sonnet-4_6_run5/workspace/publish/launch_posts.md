# Launch Posts — Ep. 14: The Podcast Production Pipeline (with Maya Chen)

---

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

Automating podcast production is smart — until it isn't. Use automation for repetitive transforms: transcription cleanup, first-draft notes, formatting. But keep a human responsible for what goes live. Make the machine produce options. Make the human approve what becomes public.
