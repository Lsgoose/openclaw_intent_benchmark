# Launch Posts

## Post 1 (Key Takeaway)
The most underrated principle of podcast production: transcripts are rich inputs, not final editorial truth. Raw transcripts contain filler, false starts, and uncorrected statements – always separate cleaned, reviewed transcripts from raw source material before generating summaries, show notes, or social content.
