Episode 14, "Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline," explores how raw recordings become polished releases with clean audio, transcripts, chapter markers, titles, descriptions, and metadata. Maya Chen breaks the workflow into five stages and explains why small teams need clear handoffs, consistent naming, separate raw and cleaned transcripts, and a fixed output bundle. The episode also covers where automation helps, where human review still matters, and how to keep titles and metadata aligned so every surface tells the same story. A practical guide for teams that want fewer mistakes and a more dependable publishing process.
