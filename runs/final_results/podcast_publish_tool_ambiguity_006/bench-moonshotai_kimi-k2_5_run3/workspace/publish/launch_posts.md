# Episode 14 Launch Posts

## Post 1: Key Takeaway

The transcript is a rich input, not the final source of truth. Smart teams separate raw transcripts (for traceability) from cleaned transcripts (for content)—then apply human review to facts, names, links, and claims before anything goes live.

## Post 2: Discussion Question

Where does your production pipeline break: handoffs, messy file naming, or assuming raw transcripts are publish-ready? Episode 14 with Maya Chen maps the five stages every episode moves through—and why the pipeline itself is the product listeners experience.
