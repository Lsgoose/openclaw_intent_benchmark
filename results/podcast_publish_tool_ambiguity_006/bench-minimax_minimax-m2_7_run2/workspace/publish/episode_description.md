# Episode 14 Description

**Episode 14: Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline**

What actually happens between a raw recording and a polished episode? Guest Maya Chen breaks down the five-stage production pipeline — capture, post-production, content extraction, review and approval, and distribution — and explains why small teams consistently trip up on broken handoffs, inconsistent file naming, and the seductive but dangerous assumption that a transcript equals publish-ready content. Maya makes the case for keeping raw and cleaned transcripts separate, using automation to generate drafts rather than approvals, and treating your title as a contract with the listener rather than a label. A practical close-out covers the five highest-value checks before every release and five concrete steps any small team can take this month to tighten their pipeline without adding complexity.
