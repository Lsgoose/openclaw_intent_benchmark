Episode 14 explores how to build a reliable podcast production pipeline that turns a raw recording into a polished release. Maya Chen walks through the five core stages: capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution. The conversation covers why small teams get stuck on handoffs, naming, and transcript cleanup, and why a cleaned transcript should be treated as a working input rather than final editorial truth. The episode also breaks down how to use automation well—speeding up drafts, formatting, and packaging without outsourcing judgment. It closes with a practical checklist for teams that want more consistency, fewer mistakes, and a smoother path from recording to publish.
