# Episode 14 — Episode Description

What actually turns a raw recording into a polished, discoverable podcast episode? In this episode, production systems advisor Maya Chen breaks down the five-stage pipeline that separates reliable shows from chaotic ones: capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution.

Maya explains why raw transcripts are a rich input but not editorial truth, how small teams can build a lightweight but consistent workflow, and where automation genuinely helps versus where human judgment is irreplaceable. The conversation covers standard output bundles, the difference between raw and cleaned transcripts, title strategy, and the five pre-publish checks that prevent the most common release mistakes.

Whether you run a two-person operation or are scaling up, this episode gives you a practical framework for building a pipeline that holds up — episode after episode.
