# Episode 14 — Launch Posts

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

Raw transcripts are a starting point, not finished copy. Filler, false starts, unverified links, and corrected statements all end up in there — treat it as clean truth and every piece of content built from it inherits those problems. Clean your transcript before you build on it.

## Post 2 — Discussion Question

Most podcast production failures happen at the handoff, not the recording. Where does your production process lose the most time or create the most confusion — editing, writing, review, or publishing?
