## Launch Post 1 (Key Takeaway)
A good podcast production pipeline doesn't just create clean audio — it aligns every asset (titles, descriptions, chapters, social copy) to deliver a consistent listener experience. Small teams don't need elaborate systems, just consistent output bundles and clear checklists.
