# Episode 14 Launch Posts

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

Automation belongs in the draft stage, not the approval stage. Use it to speed up transcription cleanup, first-draft notes, and metadata — but keep a human in the loop for everything that goes live. New episode on building a reliable podcast production pipeline.

## Post 2 — Discussion Question

At what point in your production process do you feel most confident handing off to automation — and where do you still insist on a human review? New episode on podcast production pipelines explores exactly that tension.
