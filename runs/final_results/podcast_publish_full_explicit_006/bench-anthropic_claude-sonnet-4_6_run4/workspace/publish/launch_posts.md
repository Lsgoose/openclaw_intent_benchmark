# Episode 14 Launch Posts

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

A raw transcript is not publish-ready content. It contains filler, false starts, and unverified links. Treat it as a rich first-draft input — not the final source of editorial truth. Ep 14 breaks down how to build a pipeline that catches this before it goes live.

## Post 2 — Discussion Question

Where does your podcast production pipeline break down most often — handoffs between people, inconsistent file naming, or something else entirely? Ep 14 digs into the systems that keep small teams from repeating the same production mistakes episode after episode.
