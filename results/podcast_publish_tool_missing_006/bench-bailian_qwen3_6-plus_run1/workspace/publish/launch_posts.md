# Launch Posts — Episode 14

## Post 1: Key Takeaway

A raw transcript isn't publish-ready — it has filler, false starts, and unverified claims. Keep both raw and cleaned transcripts. Use automation to accelerate drafts, not replace editorial judgment. Make the machine produce options. Make the human approve what goes public.

## Post 2: Discussion Question

Where should a podcast team draw the line between automation and human review? Transcripts can draft summaries and suggest titles, but facts, links, and sensitive edits still need a person. What have you automated, and what will you never hand off to a machine?
