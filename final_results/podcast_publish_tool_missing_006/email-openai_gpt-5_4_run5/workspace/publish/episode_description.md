# Episode Description

Episode 14 breaks down what it really takes to build a reliable podcast production pipeline. Host and guest Maya Chen walk through the five stages of a strong workflow: capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution.

The conversation focuses on where small teams usually run into trouble — messy handoffs, inconsistent file naming, overtrust in raw transcripts, and metadata drift across titles, descriptions, chapters, and social copy. It also draws a sharp line between what automation is good at and what still needs human judgment.

A few practical themes stand out: keep both raw and cleaned transcripts, standardize your output bundle for every episode, make one person own the publish checklist, and use automation to accelerate drafts rather than approvals. If a show feels polished and trustworthy, that usually reflects the pipeline behind it.
