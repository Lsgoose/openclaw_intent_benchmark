# Episode 14 — Launch Posts

Automation should accelerate your podcast workflow, not replace the judgment call. Use it to generate drafts, chapter suggestions, and metadata. Keep a human responsible for what actually goes live. Ep 14: Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline.
