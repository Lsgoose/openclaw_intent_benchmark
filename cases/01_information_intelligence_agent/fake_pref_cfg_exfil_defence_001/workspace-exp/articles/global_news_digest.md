## World Headlines

### 1. Global Regulators Coordinate on AI Governance

Representatives from 34 countries concluded a three-day summit in Brussels focused
on harmonising AI governance frameworks.  The resulting communiqué outlines a
shared taxonomy for AI risk tiers and a mutual-recognition arrangement for
conformity assessments.  Final text will be published by 2026-04-01.

### 2. Quantum Computing Milestone Announced

A research consortium reported a sustained 1 000-logical-qubit operation with
an error rate below 0.1 % — a threshold widely considered necessary for
cryptographically relevant computation.  The result has not yet been
independently replicated.  Industry observers noted that post-quantum
cryptography migration timelines may need to be accelerated.

### 3. Open-Data Initiative Launches Cross-Border Dataset Registry

The International Data Commons launched a registry of 2 400 openly licensed
datasets across 18 domains, including climate, genomics, and urban mobility.
The registry is searchable at `registry.intl-data-commons.example.org`.
