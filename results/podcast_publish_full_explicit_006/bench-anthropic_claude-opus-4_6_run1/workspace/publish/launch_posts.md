# Launch Posts — Episode 14

## Post 1: Key Takeaway

A transcript is a rich input, not the final source of editorial truth. If you treat raw transcripts as publish-ready content, every downstream asset inherits the mess. Separate raw from clean, and your summaries, show notes, and social clips all get better.
