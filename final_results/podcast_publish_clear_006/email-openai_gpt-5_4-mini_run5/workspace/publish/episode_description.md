Episode 14 explores how a reliable podcast production pipeline turns raw recordings into publish-ready episodes. Host and guest Maya Chen break down the five stages of production, explain why clean transcripts and raw transcripts should stay separate, and show how small teams can reduce errors with standard folders, naming, checklists, and a fixed output bundle. The conversation also covers title strategy, review checkpoints for names, links, sponsor language, and metadata consistency, plus the right way to use automation: accelerate drafts, but keep humans accountable for approvals and release quality.
