# Launch Posts — Episode 14

## Post 1: Key Takeaway

The biggest pipeline mistake: treating a raw transcript as final copy.

A transcript is a rich input for drafts — facts, links, names still need human eyes.

Automate options. Human approves.

## Post 2: Discussion Question

Where's your pipeline's human judgment bottleneck?

Every show has one — the step where automation can't replace a person checking something that matters. What is that step on your team?
