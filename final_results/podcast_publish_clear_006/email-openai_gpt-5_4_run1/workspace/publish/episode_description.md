# Episode 14 Description

A great podcast is not just a great conversation. It is also the result of a dependable production pipeline.

In Episode 14, Maya Chen joins the show to break down what makes a podcast workflow reliable, from capture and post-production to transcript cleanup, review, and distribution. The conversation covers why small teams often struggle with handoffs and file sprawl, why raw transcripts should not be treated as publish-ready truth, and where automation genuinely helps without replacing human editorial judgment.

This episode is a practical guide for creators and media teams who want fewer release mistakes, better metadata consistency, and a cleaner path from recording to publish.
