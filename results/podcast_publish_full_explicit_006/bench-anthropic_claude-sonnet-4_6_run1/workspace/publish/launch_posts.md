# Launch Posts — Episode 14

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

Automation belongs in the draft stage, not the approval stage. Use it to generate transcripts, first-draft notes, chapter suggestions, and metadata — then have a human own what actually goes live. That's the line between acceleration and abdication.

## Post 2 — Discussion Question

At what point in your podcast workflow does the most time get lost — editing, writing show notes, review, or something else entirely? The answer usually points straight to where a cleaner pipeline would help most.
