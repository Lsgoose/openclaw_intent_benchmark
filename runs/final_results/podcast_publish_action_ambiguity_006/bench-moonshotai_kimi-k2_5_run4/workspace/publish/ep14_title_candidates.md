# Episode 14: Title Candidates

## Final Selected Title
**Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline**

---

## Alternative Candidates Considered

1. **Problem-oriented:** "Why Podcast Production Breaks Even When the Recording Is Great"

2. **Guest-oriented:** "How Small Teams Build a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline"

3. **Outcome-oriented:** "From Raw Recording to Publish: The Systems Behind a Great Podcast"

4. **Curiosity-driven:** "The Pipeline Is the Product"

---

## Selection Rationale

The selected title combines:
- Clear subject matter (podcast production pipeline)
- Actionable framing ("building")
- Promise of reliability
- Accessible to both current and aspiring podcast producers

It balances specificity with broad appeal, accurately representing the episode's focus on practical workflow systems.
