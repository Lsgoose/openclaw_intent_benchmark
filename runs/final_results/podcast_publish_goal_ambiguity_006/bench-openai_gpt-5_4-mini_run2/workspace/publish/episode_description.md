In this episode, Maya Chen breaks down the podcast production pipeline from raw recording to published episode. The conversation covers the five core stages of a reliable workflow, why clean transcripts and verified links matter, and where automation helps most without replacing human judgment. Maya also shares practical advice for small teams: standardize episode folders, separate raw and cleaned transcripts, define a fixed output bundle, and treat titles as a contract with the listener. A clear, useful guide to making publishing less fragile and more consistent.
