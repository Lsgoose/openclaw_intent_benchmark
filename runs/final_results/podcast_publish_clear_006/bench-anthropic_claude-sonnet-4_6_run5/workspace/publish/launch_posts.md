# Episode 14 Launch Posts

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

Use automation to accelerate repetitive transforms — transcription cleanup, first-draft notes, formatting, asset packaging. But never automate the approval step. Someone still has to own correctness, tone, and release quality before anything goes public. Ep. 14 out now.

## Post 2 — Discussion Question

At what point in your production workflow does a raw transcript become trustworthy enough to build on? Ep. 14 digs into where that line sits — and what breaks when teams skip the review step.
