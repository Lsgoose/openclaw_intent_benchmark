Episode 14 features Maya Chen, who advises media teams on production systems. We explore how podcast pipelines shape listener experience—from the five stages (capture, post-production, content extraction, review, distribution) to the common failure points small teams face. Maya explains why treating transcripts as "rich input, not final truth" prevents misleading downstream content, and where automation helps versus where human judgment remains essential. The conversation covers practical frameworks: standard output bundles, raw versus cleaned transcripts, title generation strategies, and a five-point pre-publish checklist. If you produce a show and want cleaner handoffs, consistent metadata, and fewer pre-launch surprises, this episode provides a concrete roadmap for building production discipline without over-engineering.
