# Episode 14: Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

Most podcasters focus on the conversation — but the production pipeline is what actually shapes the listener experience. In this episode, media systems advisor Maya Chen breaks down the five stages of a reliable podcast pipeline: capture, post-production, content extraction, review and approval, and distribution.

Maya explains where small teams most often struggle — inconsistent handoffs, messy file naming, and the mistaken belief that a raw transcript equals publish-ready content. She makes the case for keeping raw and clean transcripts separate, defining a fixed output bundle for every episode, and using automation to accelerate repetitive tasks without giving up editorial responsibility.

The episode closes with five concrete steps any small show can take this month to build a more consistent, reliable pipeline — no elaborate system required.
