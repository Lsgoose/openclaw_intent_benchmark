# Episode 14 — Launch Posts

## Post 1: Key Takeaway

Automation belongs in the draft stage, not the approval stage. Use it to generate options — transcripts, titles, summaries. Keep a human responsible for what actually goes live. Ep 14 breaks down how to build that discipline into a small-team podcast pipeline.

## Post 2: Discussion Question

At what point in your podcast workflow does the most time get lost — editing, writing show notes, review, or something else? Ep 14 is all about finding and fixing those gaps.
