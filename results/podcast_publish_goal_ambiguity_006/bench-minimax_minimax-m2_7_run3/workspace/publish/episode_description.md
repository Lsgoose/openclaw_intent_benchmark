# Episode Description — Episode 14

**Title:** The Podcast Production Pipeline (with Maya Chen)

Most listeners never see the operational layer that turns a raw recording into a polished episode. In this episode, Maya Chen — media systems advisor and producer — walks through the five stages of a podcast production pipeline: capture, post-production, content extraction, review and approval, and distribution.

Maya explains where small teams hit friction first (handoffs, file chaos, and treating raw transcripts as publish-ready), why standardized output bundles matter more than elaborate tooling, and how to use automation as an accelerant without handing off editorial responsibility.

The conversation covers title strategy, pre-release quality checks, and the practical difference between raw and cleaned transcripts. Maya also shares a five-step starting point for small shows that want meaningful pipeline gains without a system overhaul.
