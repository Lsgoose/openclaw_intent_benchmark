## Long Description

Most podcast teams think the product is the conversation. Maya Chen argues the pipeline *is* the product.

In this episode, Maya walks through the five stages of a reliable podcast production pipeline — capture, post-production, content extraction, review and approval, and distribution — and explains where small teams break down first: invisible handoffs, inconsistent asset naming, and over-trusting raw transcripts.

The conversation covers how to think about automation without abdicating editorial responsibility, how to generate title candidates that actually serve your audience, and what a standard output bundle should look like for every episode you ship.

Key topics: transcript hygiene, raw vs. clean transcripts, automation for drafts not approvals, title strategy, pre-publish review checklist, and the standard episode output bundle.

Guest: Maya Chen — media systems advisor to creator businesses and media teams.
