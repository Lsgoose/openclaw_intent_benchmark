This episode examines the podcast production pipeline as the hidden engine behind a polished show. The conversation breaks down the full workflow from capture and post-production to transcript cleanup, content extraction, review, and distribution. It highlights why raw transcripts need human verification, how small teams can standardize handoffs, and where automation helps without replacing editorial judgment. The discussion also covers title strategy, pre-publish checks, and a simple output bundle that keeps episodes consistent. If you care about turning recordings into reliable public episodes, this one maps the process end to end.
