# Episode 14 – Launch Posts

## Post 1 – Key Takeaway

Automation belongs in the draft stage, not the approval stage. Use it to generate options — titles, summaries, chapter suggestions — but keep a human in the seat for what actually goes live. Speed without ownership is how mistakes ship. 🎙️ Ep 14 out now.

Where does your podcast production pipeline break down most often — handoffs between people, inconsistent file naming, or something else entirely? Episode 14 digs into the systems that keep small teams from dropping the ball between record and publish.
