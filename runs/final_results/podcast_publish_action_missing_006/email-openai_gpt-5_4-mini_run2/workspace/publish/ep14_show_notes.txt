Episode 14: Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

In this episode, the host and Maya Chen break down why the production pipeline shapes the listener experience just as much as the recording itself. They cover five stages—capture, post-production, content extraction, review and approval, and distribution—and explain why a reliable workflow reduces errors downstream.

Key takeaways:
- Keep raw and cleaned transcripts separate.
- Standardize the output bundle for every episode.
- Use automation for drafts and formatting, but keep approval human.
- Verify names, links, sponsor language, and metadata before publish.
- Treat transcript content as a rich input, not the final source of truth.
