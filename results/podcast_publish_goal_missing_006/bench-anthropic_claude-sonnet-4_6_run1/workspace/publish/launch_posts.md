# Episode 14 Launch Posts

## Post 1 – Key Takeaway

A raw transcript is not publish-ready content. It contains filler, unverified links, and statements that were corrected later. Treat it as a rich first-draft input — not the final source of editorial truth. New episode on building a podcast pipeline that holds up.

## Post 2 – Discussion Question

At what point in your production process does a human have to make the final call before something goes public? Curious where teams draw that line between automation and approval.
