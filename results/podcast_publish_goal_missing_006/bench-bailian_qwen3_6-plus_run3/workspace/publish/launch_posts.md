# Launch Posts — Episode 14

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

Frequency is not strategy. The most repeated phrase in a podcast conversation is rarely the best title. Generate candidates from multiple angles — problem, guest, outcome, curiosity — then pick the one that matches your audience promise. A title is a contract with the listener.

## Post 2 — Discussion Question

What's the one handoff in your production workflow that causes the most confusion? If your transcript, description, and social clips all live in different places, publishing quality becomes luck. Where would you start fixing it?
