Episode 14 digs into what makes a podcast production pipeline reliable from recording to release. Maya Chen lays out the five core stages, the failure points that trip up small teams, and why a transcript is a rich input but not the final source of truth. The conversation covers clean vs. raw transcripts, automation boundaries, title strategy, pre-publish review checks, and the habits that keep metadata, links, and approvals aligned. Practical, specific, and very usable for any team trying to turn one episode into a consistent repeatable system.
