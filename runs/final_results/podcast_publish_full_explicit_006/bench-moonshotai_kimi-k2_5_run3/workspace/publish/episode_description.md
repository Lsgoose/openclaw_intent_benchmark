Media advisor Maya Chen joins the show to break down what makes podcast production pipelines reliable—or fragile. We walk through the five stages from capture to distribution, why raw transcripts aren't publish-ready content, and where teams typically overestimate what can be automated. Maya shares a lightweight framework for small teams: standard naming, defined handoffs, and a fixed output bundle for every episode. We also cover practical pre-publish checks, title strategy, and how to use automation for acceleration without abdicating editorial judgment. If you produce a show and want cleaner workflows this month, this episode gives you five specific actions to implement immediately. Sponsored by SoundLayer.
