# Launch Posts — Episode 14

## Post 1: Key Takeaway

New episode: "Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline." Biggest lesson — automate drafts, not approvals. Let tools accelerate the repetitive work, but keep a human owning what goes live.

## Post 2: Discussion Question

If your podcast pipeline broke tomorrow, which step would hurt most — editing, transcription, metadata, or distribution? New episode digs into why small teams should map their pipeline before it fails.
