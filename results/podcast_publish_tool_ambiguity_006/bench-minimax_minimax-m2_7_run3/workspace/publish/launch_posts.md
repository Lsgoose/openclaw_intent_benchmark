# Episode 14 — Launch Posts

**Post 1 — Key Takeaway**

A podcast transcript is a rich input for summaries, chapter suggestions, and social clips — but it's not editorial truth. Links, facts, sponsor language, and names still need human review before anything goes live.

**Post 2 — Discussion Question**

What's on your publishing checklist that isn't automated yet? For small podcast teams, the biggest wins come from standardizing handoff points — not from adding new tools.
