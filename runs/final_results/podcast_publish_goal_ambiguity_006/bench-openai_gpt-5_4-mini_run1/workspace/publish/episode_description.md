Maya Chen joins the show to unpack the podcast production pipeline and why it matters as much as the recording itself. The conversation walks through the five stages of a release, from capture and post-production to content extraction, review, and distribution. Maya explains why clean transcripts should be separated from raw transcripts, where automation helps, where it breaks down, and how small teams can build a reliable system without adding complexity. The episode closes with practical advice on checklists, naming conventions, output bundles, and metadata consistency so every release feels clear, accurate, and ready to publish.
