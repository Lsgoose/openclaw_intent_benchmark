# Episode 14 — Launch Posts

---

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

Raw transcripts are not publish-ready copy. They contain filler, false starts, unverified links, and statements that were corrected two minutes later in the same conversation. Treating a transcript as clean structured truth is how low-quality or misleading content gets published. The transcript is a rich input — not the final source of editorial truth. Episode 14 out now.

## Post 2 — Discussion Question

At what point in your production process does a human have to make a call that no tool or template can make for you? Episode 14 is about building reliable podcast pipelines — and the answer to that question shapes everything about how you design one.
