# Episode 14 — Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

Every polished podcast episode rests on an invisible system: the production pipeline. In this episode, Maya Chen — who advises media teams and creator businesses on production systems — breaks down the five stages every show needs, from capture through distribution.

We explore where small teams struggle first, why raw transcripts are not publish-ready content, and how to build a lightweight but reliable pipeline with just two or three people. Maya shares practical advice on separating raw and cleaned transcripts, generating title candidates that actually serve your audience, and running a pre-publish review that catches the mistakes listeners notice immediately.

If you're experimenting with automation, the key takeaway is clear: use it to accelerate drafts, not to replace approvals. Make the machine produce options. Make the human approve what becomes public.
