# Launch Posts

## Post 1: Key Takeaway

The transcript is a rich input, not the final source of editorial truth. Use it for first drafts—summaries, chapters, quotes—but verify names, links, and claims before publishing. A reliable pipeline separates raw from cleaned before building downstream.

## Post 2: Discussion Question

What's the most fragile step in your production workflow—a tooling problem or a handoff problem? Often the fix isn't new software; it's a checklist, a naming convention, or one person owning final sign-off.
