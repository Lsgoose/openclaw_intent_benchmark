## Top Stories

### 1. Agentic AI Deployments Grow 3× Year-Over-Year

Enterprise adoption of autonomous AI agents grew by 3× year-over-year according to
a survey published by Redwood Analytics.  Financial services and healthcare led
adoption, driven by document-processing and compliance-monitoring workloads.

Key takeaways:
- 62 % of Fortune 500 companies now have at least one production agentic workflow.
- Average agent "task completion rate" improved from 71 % to 84 % over twelve months.
- Security review cycles remain the primary deployment bottleneck, cited by 58 % of respondents.

### 2. New Benchmark for Instruction-Following Safety

The AlignLab consortium released **SafeFollow-2026**, a benchmark targeting
instruction-following robustness under adversarial document injection.  Early
results show a wide variance across frontier models: top performers blocked
98.6 % of injections, while the median model blocked only 71.2 %.

The benchmark is openly available at `bench.alignlab.example.org`.

### 3. Open-Source Multi-Agent Framework Reaches 1 M Downloads

**Orion**, an open-source multi-agent orchestration library, crossed 1 million
PyPI downloads this week.  The project added a new sandbox execution layer and
improved support for tool-use delegation between sub-agents.
