# Episode 14 – Episode Description

Your podcast pipeline is the product — not just the conversation inside it.

In this episode, production systems advisor Maya Chen breaks down the five stages of a reliable podcast pipeline: capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution. She explains why small teams struggle most with handoffs, inconsistent file naming, and the false assumption that a raw transcript is publish-ready content.

Maya draws a sharp line between what automation handles well — first-draft notes, chapter suggestions, metadata prefill — and what still demands human judgment: editorial calls, exception handling, and anything that goes live under your name.

The episode closes with a practical six-step checklist any small show can act on immediately, plus a clear principle to carry forward: use automation to accelerate drafts, not to replace approvals.

If you own editing, publishing, or content ops for a podcast, this one is for you.
