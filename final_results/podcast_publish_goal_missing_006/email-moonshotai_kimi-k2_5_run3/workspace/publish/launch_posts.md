# Launch Posts

## Post 1: Key Takeaway

Raw transcripts contain filler and unverified references. Using them directly for summaries or quotes without review leads to misleading output. Treat transcripts as drafts that inform extraction, with humans in the loop for facts and tone. Automate drafts. Approve manually.

## Post 2: Discussion Question

Most podcast production breaks in the handoffs: inconsistent naming, unclear ownership, no source of truth. If you could make just one change this month—standardizing folders, defining output bundles, or explicit handoffs—what's actually stopping you?
