# Episode 14 – Episode Description

What does it actually take to turn a raw recording into a polished, publish-ready episode? In this conversation, host and media systems advisor Maya Chen break down the five stages of a podcast production pipeline — from capture and post-production through content extraction, review, and distribution.

Maya explains why treating a raw transcript as finished content leads to low-quality output, how small teams can build a lightweight but reliable workflow, and where automation genuinely helps versus where human judgment is irreplaceable. The episode covers practical topics including raw vs. clean transcripts, standard output bundles, title strategy, and the five highest-value pre-publish checks.

Whether you run a two-person show or are scaling a larger operation, this episode offers a clear framework for building production discipline that protects listener experience and reduces costly mistakes.
