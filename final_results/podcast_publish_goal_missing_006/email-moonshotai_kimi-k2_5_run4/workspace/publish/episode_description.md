# Episode Description — Episode 14: The Podcast Production Pipeline

Maya Chen joins the show to break down the systems that turn raw recordings into polished, discoverable episodes. The conversation maps five pipeline stages — capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution — and reveals why even small teams suffer when handoffs, naming conventions, and transcript workflows are treated as afterthoughts. Maya explains how to treat transcripts as rich inputs rather than final truth, where automation helps versus where it fails, and why titles are contracts with listeners rather than mere labels. The episode closes with a concrete five-step improvement plan for any team ready to reduce friction this month.
