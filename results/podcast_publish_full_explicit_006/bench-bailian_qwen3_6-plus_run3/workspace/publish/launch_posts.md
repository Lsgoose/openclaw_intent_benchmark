# Episode 14 — Launch Posts

## Post 1: Key Takeaway

A raw transcript is not publish-ready copy. It contains filler, false starts, and unverified claims. The best pipeline keeps both raw and cleaned transcripts — raw for traceability, cleaned as the source for summaries, quotes, and social content. Automate drafts, not approvals.

## Post 2: Discussion Question

Where do podcast teams most often overestimate what automation can handle? In our latest episode, we found it's usually editorial judgment and exception handling — the edge cases that make or break a release. What's been your experience?
