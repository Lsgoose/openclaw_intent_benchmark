Episode 14 explores how a reliable podcast production pipeline turns raw recordings into polished, publish-ready episodes. Maya Chen breaks down the core stages of capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution, then explains why small teams need clear naming, a standard output bundle, and a publish checklist. The conversation also covers the difference between raw and cleaned transcripts, where automation helps, where human judgment still matters, and how to create stronger titles and metadata. A practical guide for anyone trying to make podcast releases more consistent, efficient, and trustworthy.
