# Launch Posts

## Post 1: Key Takeaway

The best podcast pipelines don't eliminate human judgment—they make it easier to apply consistently. Automate transcription cleanup, first-draft summaries, and asset formatting. But keep a human in the loop for titles, claims, links, and final approvals. Edge cases break fully automated systems, and media teams live on edge cases.
