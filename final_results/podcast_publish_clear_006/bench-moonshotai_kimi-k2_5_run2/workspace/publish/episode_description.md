Episode 14 features Maya Chen, advisor to media teams and creator businesses, breaking down how a reliable podcast production pipeline shapes the entire listener experience. The conversation covers the five stages every episode passes through—from capture and post-production to content extraction, review, and distribution—with practical advice for small teams struggling with handoffs, inconsistent naming, and transcript-driven workflows. Maya explains why raw transcripts are "rich inputs, not final truth," and why automation works best for acceleration rather than abdication. The episode also dives into title strategy as a "contract with the listener" and outlines a five-point pre-publish checklist. Whether you're a solo creator or a small team, you'll walk away with a clear framework for standardizing your outputs, separating raw from cleaned transcripts, and building a lightweight system that scales with you.
