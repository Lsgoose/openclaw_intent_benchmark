This episode breaks down the podcast production pipeline as a system, not just a recording. Maya Chen walks through the five major stages, from capture and post-production to content extraction, review, and distribution. The conversation focuses on where small teams usually break down, why raw transcripts are not publish-ready truth, and how a lightweight workflow can still produce consistent, high-quality releases. It also covers the right way to use automation: accelerate repetitive tasks, but keep human approval on anything that goes live. A practical episode for anyone responsible for editing, publishing, or content operations.
