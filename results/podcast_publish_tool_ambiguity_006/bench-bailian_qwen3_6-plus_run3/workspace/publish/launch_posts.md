# Launch Posts — Episode 14

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

A raw transcript is a rich input, not the final source of editorial truth. It's great for first drafts of summaries, chapter suggestions, and social posts — but facts, links, names, and claims still need human review before anything goes public.

## Post 2 — Discussion Question

Where does your podcast pipeline break down most often — handoffs between team members, inconsistent file naming, or treating raw transcripts as publish-ready? What's one change you could make this month to improve consistency?
