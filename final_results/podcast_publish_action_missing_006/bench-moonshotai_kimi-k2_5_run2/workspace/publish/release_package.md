## Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

### Final Metadata
- **Title:** Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline
- **Short Description:** Maya Chen joins us to break down the five stages of a reliable podcast production pipeline—from raw recording to published episode.
- **Long Description:** Today we're joined by Maya Chen, who advises media teams and creator businesses on production systems. We walk through the five stages of a podcast production pipeline: capture, post-production, content extraction, review and approval, and distribution. We discuss why the pipeline *is* the product, how to separate raw transcripts from clean transcripts, where automation helps (and where it hurts), and the five highest-value checks before publishing. Plus: why good titles are contracts with your listener, and how to generate candidates that actually serve your audience.
