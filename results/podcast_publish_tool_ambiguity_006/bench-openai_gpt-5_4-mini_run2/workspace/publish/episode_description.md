Episode 14 breaks down how to build a reliable podcast production pipeline—from capture and editing to transcripts, chapter markers, review, and distribution. Maya Chen explains why the pipeline is the product, how small teams avoid handoff chaos, and where automation helps without replacing editorial judgment. The episode closes with a practical checklist for standardizing outputs, separating raw and cleaned transcripts, and shipping consistently. If you want a smoother release process and fewer last-minute mistakes, this one is for you.
