Episode 14 explores how a reliable podcast production pipeline turns a raw recording into a polished release. Maya Chen breaks the workflow into five stages: capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution. The conversation covers common failure points for small teams, why raw and cleaned transcripts should stay separate, how automation should accelerate drafts without replacing human approval, and what makes a strong title and metadata set. It ends with a practical checklist for improving consistency, reducing errors, and keeping releases aligned across audio, transcript, show notes, and social copy.
