# Episode 15 — Title Tests

Click-through rate is a metric, not a strategy. In this episode, Jordan Kim explains why podcast teams keep running title experiments that teach them nothing — and what to do instead.

The conversation covers the difference between testing for novelty and testing for audience fit, what a useful title test log actually records, and why reusing a winning headline without its original context is one of the most common mistakes Jordan sees week after week.

Jordan also shares a practical starting point for small teams: begin an experiment log this month, write down the audience promise for each title option, and stop treating CTR as the whole picture.

This episode is for producers, network ops teams, and anyone responsible for getting the right listeners to the right episode.

Length: 3 minutes.
Guest: Jordan Kim — packaging reviews and title experiments for podcast networks.
