# Episode 14 – Launch Posts

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

A raw transcript is not publish-ready content. It contains filler, false starts, unverified links, and statements that were corrected later. Treating it as clean structured truth is how low-quality derivative content gets published. Separate raw from cleaned — every time.

## Post 2 — Discussion Question

Most podcast teams automate drafts but still manually approve what goes live. Where do you draw that line? What's the last thing in your publishing workflow you'd trust a machine to approve on its own?
