# Episode 14 – Launch Posts

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

A raw transcript is not publish-ready copy. It contains filler, unverified links, and statements that were corrected two minutes later. The transcript is a rich input — not the final source of editorial truth. New episode on building a podcast pipeline that holds up.

At what stage does your podcast production most often break down — handoffs between people, inconsistent file naming, or the gap between raw transcript and finished copy? New episode unpacking the full pipeline from recording to publish.
