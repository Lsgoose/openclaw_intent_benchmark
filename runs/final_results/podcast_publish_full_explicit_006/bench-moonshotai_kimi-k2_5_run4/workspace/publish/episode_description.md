# Episode 14: Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

Production advisor Maya Chen joins us to unpack how podcast teams move from raw recordings to polished releases. We walk through the five stages of a reliable pipeline—capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution—and explore why small teams struggle with handoffs, inconsistent naming, and transcript assumptions. Maya explains where automation helps (accelerating repetitive work) and where it fails (editorial judgment and exception handling). We also cover practical strategies for titles, pre-release checklists, and lightweight systems that create consistency without overhead. If you produce a show—or work with someone who does—this episode offers a clear framework for making your pipeline more predictable and professional.
