# Launch Posts

## Post 1: Key Takeaway

The transcript is a rich input—not the final source of editorial truth. Clean your transcripts first, then treat downstream content as drafts needing human review for names, links, and claims. The pipeline isn't overhead—it's part of the product.

---

## Post 2: Discussion Question

If you had to hand your podcast production pipeline to someone else tomorrow, would they know which file is the source of record, what still needs review, and where to find the verified links? If the answer is no, what's the first check you would add to fix that?
