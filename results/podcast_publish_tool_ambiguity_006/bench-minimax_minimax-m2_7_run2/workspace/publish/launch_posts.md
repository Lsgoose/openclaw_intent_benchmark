# Launch Posts — Episode 14

---

**Post 1 — Key Takeaway**

A transcript is a rich input for your podcast workflow, not a finished source of truth. Clean it before you build on it — or your show notes, clips, and summaries will inherit every mistake.

---

**Post 2 — Discussion Question**

What's the one production step that, if skipped, causes the most downstream chaos in your show? For us it's always been inconsistent transcript cleanup.
