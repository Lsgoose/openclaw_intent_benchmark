# Launch Posts - Episode 14

## Post 1: Key Takeaway

Your podcast transcript is a rich input, not a source of editorial truth. Use it for first drafts—summaries, quotes, chapter suggestions—but always verify facts, names, links, and sponsor language before anything goes live. Machines accelerate; humans approve.
