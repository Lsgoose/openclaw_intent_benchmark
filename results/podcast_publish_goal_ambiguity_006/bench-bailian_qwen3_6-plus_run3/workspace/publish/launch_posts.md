# Launch Posts

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

The pipeline is the product. Titles, transcripts, chapter markers, and publishing consistency shape the listener experience just as much as the conversation itself. Small teams don't need more AI — they need fewer invisible handoffs and a standard output bundle for every episode.

## Post 2 — Discussion Question

If you run a small podcast and could only change one thing about your production workflow this month, what would it be? Defining a folder structure, creating a publish checklist, separating raw and cleaned transcripts, or standardizing your output bundle?
