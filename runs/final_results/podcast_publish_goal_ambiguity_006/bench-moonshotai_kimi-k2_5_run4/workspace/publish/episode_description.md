# Episode Description

Maya Chen joins the show to unpack the hidden infrastructure behind great podcasts: the production pipeline. What happens after the microphones turn off? Maya breaks the process into five stages — capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution — and explains why even small teams need production discipline. We discuss the difference between raw and cleaned transcripts, where automation helps and where it fails, and why your episode title is a contract with the listener. If you've ever struggled with handoffs, inconsistent assets, or the gap between a recording and a polished release, this episode provides a practical framework you can implement immediately. Maya also shares a five-point checklist to improve any pipeline this month, regardless of team size.
