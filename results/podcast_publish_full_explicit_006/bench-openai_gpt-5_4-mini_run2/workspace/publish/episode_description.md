Episode 14 explores how a reliable podcast production pipeline turns a raw recording into a polished release. Maya Chen breaks down the five stages of the workflow, from capture and post-production to content extraction, review, and distribution. The conversation covers why clean transcripts matter, how small teams avoid handoff problems, and where automation helps without replacing human judgment. The key idea is simple: transcripts, titles, descriptions, chapter markers, and metadata all need to align if the episode is going to feel trustworthy and easy to discover. The episode closes with practical guidance for small teams that want better consistency without building an overcomplicated system.
