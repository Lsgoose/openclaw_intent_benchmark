# Episode 14: Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

What separates a polished podcast from a chaotic one isn't just the quality of the conversation — it's the production pipeline behind it. In this episode, host and media systems advisor Maya Chen walk through the five stages of a reliable podcast pipeline: capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution.

Maya explains why raw transcripts are a rich input but not a final source of truth, how small teams can standardize their output bundle to reduce errors, and where automation genuinely helps versus where human judgment is irreplaceable. The conversation covers title strategy, the five highest-value pre-publish checks, and a practical five-step plan for improving your pipeline this month.

Whether you're a solo creator or a small team, this episode gives you a clear framework for building consistency into every release — so the pipeline stops being the thing that breaks and starts being the thing that scales.
