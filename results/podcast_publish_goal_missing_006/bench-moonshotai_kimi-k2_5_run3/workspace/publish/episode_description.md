# Episode Description

Why do title tests fail even when the numbers look good? In this episode, Jordan Kim joins the show to break down how teams mistake click-through rates for strategy—and why tracking novelty instead of audience promise leads to hollow wins.

You'll learn how to build a lightweight experiment log, set up a healthy review workflow, and avoid the common trap of recycling winning titles into the wrong contexts. Whether you're running tests for a network or just trying to sharpen your episode headlines, this conversation offers practical frameworks for making title testing actually useful.
