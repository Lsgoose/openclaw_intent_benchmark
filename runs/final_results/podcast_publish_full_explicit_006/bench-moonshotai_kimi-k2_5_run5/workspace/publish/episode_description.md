Episode 14 features media systems advisor Maya Chen on building podcast production pipelines that actually work. We walk through the five stages—from capture and post-production to content extraction, review, and distribution—and explore why even small teams need production discipline. Maya explains the critical difference between raw and cleaned transcripts, where automation helps versus where it fails, and how to create titles that serve as genuine contracts with listeners. You'll come away with a practical checklist for pre-publish review and five concrete steps to tighten your pipeline this month without adding overhead.
