# Episode 14 Launch Posts

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

A raw transcript is not publish-ready copy. It contains filler, false starts, unverified links, and statements that were corrected later. Separating raw from cleaned material is one of the highest-leverage moves a small podcast team can make. Ep 14 out now.

## Post 2 — Discussion Question

Most podcast production failures happen in the handoffs, not the recording. Where does your show's pipeline tend to break down — editing, writing, review, or getting everything out the door consistently? Ep 14 digs into this.
