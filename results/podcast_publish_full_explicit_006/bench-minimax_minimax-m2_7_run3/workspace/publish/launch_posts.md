# Episode 14 — Launch Posts

**Post 1 — Key Takeaway**

A transcript is a rich input, not final editorial truth. Use it to draft summaries, chapter suggestions, and quote candidates — but links, facts, sponsor language, and anything authoritative still need human review before it goes live.

**Post 2 — Discussion Question**

What's the first thing that breaks in your podcast workflow — handoffs, file naming, or transcript chaos? Getting that right shapes everything downstream.
