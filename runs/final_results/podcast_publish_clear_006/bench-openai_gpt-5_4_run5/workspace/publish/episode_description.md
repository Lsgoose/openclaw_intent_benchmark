# Episode Description

Episode 14 explores how small teams can build a reliable podcast production pipeline without overcomplicating the process. Host and guest Maya Chen break the workflow into five stages: capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution. They explain why the pipeline is part of the product, where small teams usually fail first, and why raw transcripts should never be treated as final editorial truth.

The episode also covers the difference between raw and cleaned transcripts, where automation helps versus where human judgment still matters, how to think about episode titles, and the highest-value checks before publishing. The practical takeaway: standardize folder structure, naming, outputs, and review steps before trying to automate everything.
