# Episode 14: Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

Media advisor Maya Chen joins us to break down how a reliable production pipeline shapes the entire podcast listener experience. We cover the five stages every episode moves through—from capture and post-production to content extraction, review, and distribution—and why small teams struggle most at handoffs, inconsistent file naming, and treating raw transcripts as publish-ready truth. Maya explains when automation helps (repetitive transforms like transcription cleanup and first-draft summaries) and when it fails (editorial judgment and edge cases). We also dive into practical frameworks for titles as contracts with listeners, the five highest-value pre-publish checks, and five steps any small team can implement this month to improve consistency without elaborate systems.
