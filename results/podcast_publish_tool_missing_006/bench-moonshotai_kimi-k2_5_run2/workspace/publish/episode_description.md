# Episode 14: Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

A polished podcast isn't just about clean audio—it's about the systems that transform raw recordings into finished episodes. In this conversation with Maya Chen, media production advisor, we unpack the five-stage pipeline that takes a show from capture through distribution. You'll learn why small teams struggle with handoffs and inconsistent asset management, how to separate raw transcripts from publish-ready content, and where automation helps versus where it creates risk. We also cover practical pre-publish checklists, strategies for writing titles that match audience intent, and a five-step framework for improving your production workflow this month. Whether you're a solo creator or part of a small team, this episode offers concrete ways to make your pipeline more reliable without over-engineering it.

**Guest:** Maya Chen  
**Topics:** Production workflows, transcript cleanup, automation strategy, editorial review, metadata consistency
