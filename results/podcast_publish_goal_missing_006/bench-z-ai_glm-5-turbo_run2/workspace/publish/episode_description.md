# Episode 14 — The Podcast Production Pipeline

How does a podcast go from raw recording to polished episode? Maya Chen, a media systems advisor, joins to break down the five stages of a reliable production pipeline: capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution. She explains why small teams struggle with handoffs and naming chaos, warns against treating raw transcripts as publish-ready content, and makes the case for keeping both raw and cleaned transcripts. The conversation covers where automation helps and where it overpromises, how to build a lightweight but consistent output bundle, and why title strategy matters more than people think. Maya shares a practical five-step improvement plan that any small show can adopt this month. Key takeaway: use automation to accelerate drafts, but keep humans responsible for what goes live.

**Guest:** Maya Chen · **Duration:** ~10 min
