# Episode 14 Launch Posts

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

A raw transcript is a rich input, not the final source of editorial truth. Ep 14 of the podcast breaks down how to build a production pipeline that keeps quality high from recording to publish.

## Post 2 — Discussion Question

Where does your podcast production pipeline break down most often — handoffs, naming conventions, transcript quality, or pre-publish review? Ep 14 digs into the systems that keep small shows consistent.
