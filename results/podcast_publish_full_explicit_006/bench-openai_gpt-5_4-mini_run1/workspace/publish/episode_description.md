Episode 14 explores how a reliable podcast production pipeline turns a good conversation into a polished release. Maya Chen outlines the five core stages: capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution. The episode focuses on the practical systems that keep small teams consistent: clear folder structures, naming conventions, separate raw and cleaned transcripts, standardized output bundles, and a publish checklist. It also covers where automation helps most, where human judgment still matters, and how to build titles, metadata, and social copy that stay aligned. The central message is simple: automate drafts, not approvals, and make consistency the foundation of release quality.
