# Episode 14: Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

Production systems expert Maya Chen joins the show to explain why your podcast pipeline is the product—not just the conversation. The episode breaks down the five stages of production (capture, post-production, content extraction, review, distribution) and reveals where small teams typically struggle: handoffs, inconsistent naming, and treating raw transcripts as publish-ready copy. Maya shares practical strategies for lightweight but reliable workflows, including the critical distinction between raw and cleaned transcripts, why automation should accelerate rather than replace human judgment, and how to generate titles that serve as contracts with your listeners. The discussion covers the five essential pre-publish checks—names, links, sponsor compliance, sensitive edits, and metadata consistency—and offers a concrete action plan for teams ready to improve their pipeline this month. Whether you're running a two-person show or scaling a media operation, this episode provides the framework for building production discipline that actually improves the listener experience.
