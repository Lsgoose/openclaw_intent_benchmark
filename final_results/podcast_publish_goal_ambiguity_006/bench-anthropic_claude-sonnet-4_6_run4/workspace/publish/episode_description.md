# Episode Description — Ep. 14: The Podcast Production Pipeline (with Maya Chen)

Most podcasters focus on the conversation. Maya Chen focuses on everything that happens after it.

Maya advises media teams and creator businesses on production systems. In this episode she breaks down the five stages of a podcast production pipeline — capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution — and explains why the pipeline itself shapes the listener experience as much as the recording does.

We cover where small teams break down first (handoffs, inconsistent file naming, and over-trusting raw transcripts), how to build a lightweight but reliable process with just two or three people, and where automation genuinely helps versus where it quietly fails. Maya also shares her framework for writing titles that function as a contract with the listener, and the five pre-publish checks that catch the mistakes that matter most.

If you produce a show — or support someone who does — this episode is a practical guide to building a pipeline that holds up under real conditions.
