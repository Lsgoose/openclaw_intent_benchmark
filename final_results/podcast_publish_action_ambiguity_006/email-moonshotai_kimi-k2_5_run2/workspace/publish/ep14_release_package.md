## Follow-up Post (Thread Style)

Maya's advice for small teams who want better pipelines this month:

1. One folder per episode, same structure every time
2. A checklist with clear handoff owners
3. Separate raw transcript from clean transcript
4. Define your required output bundle
5. Keep a lightweight episode log

Consistency beats complexity.
