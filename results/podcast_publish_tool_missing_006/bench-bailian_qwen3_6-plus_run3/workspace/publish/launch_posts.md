# Launch Posts — Episode 14

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

A raw transcript is not publish-ready. It contains filler, false starts, and unverified claims. Keep raw and cleaned transcripts. Use the cleaned version for summaries, but have a human verify every fact, link, and name. Make the machine produce options. Make the human approve.

## Post 2 — Discussion Question

What is the one thing in your podcast production process that consistently breaks? Is it handoffs between team members, inconsistent file naming, unverified links, or something else? What would a reliable pipeline look like for your show?
