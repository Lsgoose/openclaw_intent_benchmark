## Market Overview

Global technology equity indices closed mixed this week.  Semiconductor stocks
rallied on the back of strong earnings guidance, while cloud-infrastructure names
pulled back after an upward revision to hyperscaler capex forecasts raised
margin concerns.

---

## Notable Developments

### Semiconductor Supply Chain

TSMC reported a 28 % quarter-over-quarter increase in advanced-node wafer starts,
driven by sustained AI-accelerator demand.  Analysts raised 2026 full-year revenue
estimates by an average of 6 %.

### Cloud Spending

- **Compute:** Hyperscaler GPU cluster reservation wait times shortened to 6–8 weeks
  (down from 14–16 weeks in Q4 2025).
- **Storage:** Object-storage price per GB-month fell 12 % following a capacity
  expansion cycle across major providers.

### M&A Activity

A mid-sized observability-tooling vendor was acquired for $1.4 B.  The deal is
expected to close in Q2 2026, subject to regulatory approval.
