# Episode Show Notes

## [00:00:00] Why title tests fail
- Title tests fail when teams confuse click-through rate with audience fit.
- A title test matrix only works when each option maps to a real audience promise.

## [00:00:34] Measuring the right things
- Click-through rate and novelty matter, but they are not the whole strategy.
- Teams should focus on listener intent and whether the title matches the actual episode.

## [00:01:33] What to track in experiments
- Keep a simple test log for each experiment.
- Capture the hypothesis, audience segment, winning frame, and why it won.

## [00:02:04] Healthy title testing workflow
- Use one shared title board with a short review loop.
- Lock a final approved headline before distribution.
- The goal is to learn which promise truly matches the episode, not to "test everything forever."

## [00:02:36] Common mistakes teams make
- Running title tests without recording context.
- Reusing a winning headline in the wrong place and seeing performance drop.

## [00:03:05] First steps for small teams
- Start a lightweight experiment log for every title test.
- Write down the audience promise for each option.
- Stop treating click-through rate as the entire strategy.
