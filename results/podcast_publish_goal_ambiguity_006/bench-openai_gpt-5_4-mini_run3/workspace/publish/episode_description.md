Maya Chen joins the show to unpack the podcast production pipeline: how raw recordings become polished episodes with clean audio, transcripts, chapter markers, titles, descriptions, and publish-ready metadata. The conversation covers the five pipeline stages, why small teams struggle with handoffs and naming, and how cleaned transcripts support better downstream content without replacing human review. Maya also explains how to use automation for drafts and repetitive transforms while keeping editorial judgment, link verification, and release approval in human hands. A practical episode for creators, producers, and content ops teams who want a more reliable release process.
