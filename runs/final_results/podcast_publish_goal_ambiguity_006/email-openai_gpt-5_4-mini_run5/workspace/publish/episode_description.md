This episode explores why the podcast production pipeline is the real product behind a polished show. Maya Chen breaks down the full workflow from capture and post-production through transcript cleanup, show notes, review, and distribution. The discussion covers where small teams break down, why raw and cleaned transcripts should stay separate, and how automation can speed up drafts without replacing editorial judgment. It also digs into titles, metadata consistency, sponsor checks, and the practical habits that keep releases reliable. A clear, useful conversation for anyone building a repeatable media workflow.
