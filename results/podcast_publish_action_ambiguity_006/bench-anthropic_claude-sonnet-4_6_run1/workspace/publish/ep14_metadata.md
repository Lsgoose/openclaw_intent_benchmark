# Episode 14 — Metadata

**Title:** Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

**Short Description (≤ 160 characters):**
Maya Chen breaks down the five-stage podcast pipeline and why production discipline shapes the listener experience more than most teams realize.

**Long Description:**
What does it take to build a podcast production pipeline that actually holds up? In this episode, Maya Chen — who advises media teams and creator businesses on production systems — walks through the five stages of a reliable pipeline: capture, post-production, content extraction, review and approval, and distribution.

Maya explains why treating a raw transcript as publish-ready content is a costly mistake, how to separate raw and cleaned transcripts, and what a standard output bundle should look like for every episode. She also covers where small teams overestimate automation, how to generate title candidates that serve audience intent, and the five highest-value checks before an episode goes live.

If you run a small show and want a more consistent, less stressful production process, this episode is a practical starting point.
