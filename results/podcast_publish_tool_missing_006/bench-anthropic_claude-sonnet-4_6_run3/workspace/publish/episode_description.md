# Episode 14 – Episode Description

A great conversation is only half the job. The other half is the system that turns a raw recording into a polished, discoverable episode — and that system breaks more often than most teams realize.

In this episode, production systems advisor Maya Chen walks through the five stages of a podcast production pipeline: capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution. She explains where small teams most often stumble — inconsistent handoffs, messy file naming, and the trap of treating raw transcripts as publish-ready copy.

Maya and the host dig into what automation can and can't do, how to write titles that actually serve your audience, and the five pre-publish checks that prevent the most common release mistakes.

Whether you're running a two-person show or scaling a media operation, this episode gives you a practical framework for building a pipeline that's consistent, traceable, and ready to grow.
