In this Pipeline episode, we dig into why so many title tests fail and what to do instead. Host and guest Jordan Kim break down the difference between chasing click-through rate and actually matching your episode to the right audience. 

We walk through how to design a useful title test matrix, what metrics to watch beyond CTR, and why listener intent should guide every experiment. Jordan shares a simple framework for keeping a test log that your whole team can use, plus a practical title workflow built around one shared board, a tight review loop, and a clear final headline before distribution.

If you’ve ever felt like title testing turned into a slot machine, this conversation will help you turn experiments back into a learning engine instead of a gamble.
