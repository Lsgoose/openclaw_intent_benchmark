# Episode 14: Episode Description — Podcast Production Pipeline with Maya Chen

What happens between pressing "stop record" and pressing "publish"? In this episode, production systems advisor Maya Chen walks through the five stages of a podcast production pipeline—capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution—and explains why each one shapes the listener experience more than most teams realize.

Maya and the host discuss where small teams break down (handoffs, file naming, and treating raw transcripts as finished content), how to build a lightweight but reliable workflow, and where automation helps versus where it creates new risks. They cover title strategy, pre-publish review checklists, and the difference between raw and clean transcripts.

If you run a small show and want to tighten your production process, this episode gives you a concrete five-step starting point.
