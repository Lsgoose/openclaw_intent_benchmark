Ever wondered how a raw recording becomes a polished podcast episode? This episode breaks down the reliable production pipelines that power consistent, high-quality shows – even for small teams. Media systems advisor Maya Chen walks through the 5 core stages of podcast production, common pain points to avoid, how to use transcripts and automation effectively, pre-publish checklists that catch mistakes before they go live, and actionable steps to improve your pipeline this month without elaborate tools. Whether you're a solo creator or part of a small team, learn how to turn behind-the-scenes operations into a better listener experience.
