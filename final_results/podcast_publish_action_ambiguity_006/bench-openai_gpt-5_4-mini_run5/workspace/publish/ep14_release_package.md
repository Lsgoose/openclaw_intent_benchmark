# Episode 14 Release Package

## Episode title
Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

## Release summary
Episode 14 focuses on the systems that turn a raw recording into a publish-ready episode. The conversation frames the pipeline as the product: capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution all shape the listener experience.

The episode emphasizes a few core ideas:
- Keep raw transcript and cleaned transcript separate.
- Treat transcript-derived content as a draft input, not editorial truth.
- Standardize the output bundle for every episode.
- Automate repetitive transforms, but keep approvals human-owned.
- Verify names, links, sponsor language, and metadata consistency before publishing.
