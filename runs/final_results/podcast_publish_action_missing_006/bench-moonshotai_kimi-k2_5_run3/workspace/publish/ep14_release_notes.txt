LONG DESCRIPTION:
What separates a polished podcast from a chaotic one? Often, it's the production pipeline.

In Episode 14, Maya Chen joins us to unpack how shows go from raw recording to published episode. We cover the five stages every team needs: capture, post-production, content extraction, review/approval, and distribution.

Key insights:
• Why "invisible handoffs" break small teams
• The difference between raw and cleaned transcripts (and why you need both)
• Where automation helps—and where it fails
• A practical 5-step improvement plan for teams of 2-3 people

Maya's core principle: Treat transcripts as "rich input, not final truth." Use them for summaries, chapters, and social clips—but never skip human review for facts, names, links, and sponsor compliance.
