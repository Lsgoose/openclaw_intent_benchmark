# Episode 14: Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline — Episode Description

What separates a polished podcast from one that feels inconsistent? Usually it's not the conversation itself — it's the production pipeline behind it.

In this episode, production systems advisor Maya Chen breaks down the five stages every podcast moves through: capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution. She explains where small teams typically struggle — messy handoffs, inconsistent naming, and treating raw transcripts as publish-ready content — and offers a practical framework for building reliability without heavy tooling.

Topics include separating raw and clean transcripts, designing a standardized output bundle, knowing where automation helps versus where editorial judgment still matters, crafting better episode titles, and running a pre-publish review checklist that catches real problems.

Whether you produce solo or with a small team, this episode gives you a concrete path to fewer mistakes and more consistent releases.
