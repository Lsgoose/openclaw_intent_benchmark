Episode 14: Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

What separates a good podcast from a great one often isn't the conversation itself—it's the production pipeline behind it. In this episode, media systems advisor Maya Chen joins us to unpack the five stages of a reliable podcast workflow: capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution.

We explore why raw transcripts aren't publish-ready content, how small teams can build lightweight but robust systems, and where automation helps versus where human judgment remains essential. Maya shares practical frameworks for title generation, pre-publish checklists, and managing the handoffs that often break down in creator workflows.

Key insight: treat your transcript as "a rich input, not the final source of editorial truth." Whether you're a solo creator or part of a small team, this episode offers actionable steps to reduce errors, improve consistency, and free up mental bandwidth for the creative work that matters most.
