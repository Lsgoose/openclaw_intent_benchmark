# Launch Posts — Episode 14

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

A raw transcript is a rich input, not the final source of editorial truth. It contains filler, false starts, and unverified references. Use it to draft summaries and social copy — but always have a human review facts, links, names, and sponsor language before publishing.

## Post 2 — Discussion Question

What's the one bottleneck in your podcast production pipeline that causes the most delays — handoffs between team members, inconsistent file naming, transcript cleanup, title selection, or pre-publish review? Where would a small improvement make the biggest difference?
