# Launch Posts — Episode 14

## Post 1: Key Takeaway

A transcript is a rich input, not the final source of editorial truth. Automate your drafts — summaries, chapters, metadata — but never automate approvals. The machine produces options. The human owns what goes public.

What's the first thing that breaks in your podcast production workflow when you're short on time — file naming, transcript cleanup, or the publish checklist? Where does quality slip first?
