# Episode 14 Show Notes

## Title
Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

## Description
What turns a raw recording into a polished, trustworthy episode? In Episode 14, we unpack the podcast production pipeline from capture and post-production through content extraction, review, and distribution. Maya Chen joins the show to explain why small teams run into trouble with handoffs, inconsistent naming, unreviewed transcripts, and metadata drift—and what a lightweight, reliable workflow actually looks like.

We also dig into where automation helps, where it still needs human oversight, and why a cleaned transcript should be treated as a better working source than raw transcription output.
