# Launch Post 1 (Key Takeaway)
The biggest mistake small podcast teams make with automation? Using it to replace editorial judgment instead of accelerating repetitive work. Automate drafts, formatting, and metadata prefill – but always have a human own what goes live.

# Launch Post 2 (Discussion Question)
What's the most frequent point of failure in your podcast production pipeline? Handoffs between teammates? Version control chaos? Inconsistent metadata across platforms?
