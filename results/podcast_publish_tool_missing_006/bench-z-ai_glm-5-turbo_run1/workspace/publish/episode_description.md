# Episode 14: Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

Maya Chen joins the show to break down the five stages every podcast needs — capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution — and explain why the pipeline itself is the product. She covers where small teams stumble first (handoffs, naming chaos, treating transcripts as finished content), how to separate raw and cleaned transcripts, and where automation helps versus where it overpromises. The conversation includes practical frameworks for title strategy, pre-publish checklists, and building a consistent output bundle that makes every downstream task easier. Whether you're a solo creator or a small media team, this episode offers a clear roadmap to production discipline without over-engineering.
