# Episode 14 Description

A reliable production pipeline doesn't just save time—it protects the listener experience. In this episode, media systems advisor Maya Chen breaks down the five stages of podcast production, from capture through distribution, and explains why small teams fail at handoffs, asset management, and transcript assumptions. We explore the boundary between automation and human judgment: where AI can accelerate drafts (summaries, chapter suggestions, social copy) and where editorial oversight remains essential (names, links, sponsor compliance, sensitive edits). Maya shares practical frameworks for folder structure, naming conventions, output bundles, and review checklists that any team can implement this month. Whether you're wrestling with version control chaos or deciding what to automate first, this conversation offers a sustainable approach to production discipline without enterprise-level overhead.
