# Episode 14 – Launch Posts

---

## Post 1 – Key Takeaway

Raw transcripts are not publish-ready content. They contain filler, false starts, and unverified links. Keep a raw version for traceability and a clean version for repurposing. Never skip human review before going live. Episode 14 breaks down the production pipeline and where small teams go wrong.

---

## Post 2 – Discussion Question

Where in your podcast production process does human judgment become non-negotiable — and where has automation made things worse before better? Episode 14 explores that tension: where to let tools accelerate the work, and where to keep a person in the loop before publish.
