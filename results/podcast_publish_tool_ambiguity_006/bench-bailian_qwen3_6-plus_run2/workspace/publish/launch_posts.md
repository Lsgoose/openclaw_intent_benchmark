# Launch Posts — Episode 14

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

The transcript is a rich input, not the final source of editorial truth. Raw transcripts contain filler, false starts, and unverified links. Clean them before generating summaries or titles. Make the machine produce options. Make the human approve what goes public.

## Post 2 — Discussion Question

What's the one thing in your podcast production process that still depends on someone's memory instead of a checklist? If you had to standardize one output for every episode — audio, transcript, show notes, or metadata — which would you pick first and why?
