## Short Description (≤ 120 characters)

Maya Chen on the five-stage pipeline that turns a raw recording into a polished, publish-ready episode.

## Long Description

Most podcasters focus on the conversation. Maya Chen focuses on what happens after — and argues that the pipeline is the product. In this episode, she walks through the five stages of a reliable production workflow: capture, post-production, content extraction, review and approval, and distribution. She explains why transcripts are rich inputs but not editorial truth, where small teams break down, and how to use automation without giving up responsibility for what goes live. Practical, systems-minded, and immediately applicable for any team running a show.
