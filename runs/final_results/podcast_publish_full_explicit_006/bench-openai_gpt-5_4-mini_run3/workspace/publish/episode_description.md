Episode 14 explores how to build a reliable podcast production pipeline from raw recording to publish. Maya Chen breaks down the five core stages, common failure points in small teams, and why a cleaned transcript should sit beside the raw one. The conversation also covers automation boundaries, title strategy, review checks, and the practical habits that keep metadata, assets, and approvals aligned. If you want a smaller show to feel more professional — without adding unnecessary complexity — this episode is a clear roadmap.
