Episode 14: Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

What separates amateur podcasts from professional productions? The pipeline. Maya Chen joins us to break down the five stages every show needs—from capture and post-production to review and distribution. We explore why raw transcripts aren't publish-ready copy, how small teams can build lightweight but reliable systems, and where automation helps versus where it fails. You'll learn practical steps to fix your pipeline this month: standardize your folder structure, separate raw and cleaned transcripts, create a publish checklist, and define a fixed output bundle for every episode. If you've ever lost track of which file is the "real final" version—or wondered why your social clips feel off—this episode is for you.
