# Episode 14 — Description

## Short Description

Maya Chen breaks down the five stages of a reliable podcast production pipeline and explains why small teams should automate drafts, not approvals.

## Long Description

How does a raw recording become a polished episode with clean audio, a transcript, chapter markers, social clips, and accurate metadata? In this episode, Maya Chen walks through the five stages of a podcast production pipeline — capture, post-production, content extraction, review and approval, and distribution — and explains where small teams typically break down.

Maya shares practical advice on separating raw and clean transcripts, generating strong title candidates, running high-value pre-publish checks, and building a standard output bundle that keeps every episode consistent. Her core principle: let automation accelerate repetitive transforms, but keep a human responsible for what goes live.
