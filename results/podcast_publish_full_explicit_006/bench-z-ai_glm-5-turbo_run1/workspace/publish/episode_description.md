# Episode 14: Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

In this episode, Maya Chen joins us to break down the five stages of a podcast production pipeline — from capture and post-production through content extraction, review, and distribution. We explore why small teams struggle with handoffs, inconsistent asset naming, and the common mistake of treating raw transcripts as publish-ready content.

Maya explains when automation accelerates your workflow and when it overreaches, covering title strategy, the critical difference between raw and cleaned transcripts, and five high-value pre-publish checks every episode should pass. Whether you run a two-person show or a growing media operation, this episode offers a practical roadmap to a more consistent, reliable production system.

Key takeaway: automate drafts, not approvals. Make the machine produce options; make a human own what goes live.
