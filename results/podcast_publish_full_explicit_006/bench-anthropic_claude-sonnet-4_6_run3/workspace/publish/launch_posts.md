# Episode 14 Launch Posts

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

A raw transcript is not publish-ready content. It contains filler, unverified links, and corrections made later in the conversation. Treat it as a rich first-draft input — then apply human review before anything goes public. Ep 14 of the podcast breaks down why.

## Post 2 — Discussion Question

Where does your podcast production pipeline break down most often — handoffs between team members, inconsistent file naming, or something else entirely? Ep 14 digs into the systems behind reliable show production.
