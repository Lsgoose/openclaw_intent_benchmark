# Episode 14 Launch Posts

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

Automation belongs in the draft stage, not the approval stage. Use it to generate options — transcripts, titles, summaries — but keep a human in the loop before anything goes public. That's how small teams stay fast without losing quality.

## Post 2 — Discussion Question

When something goes wrong in a podcast release — wrong name, broken link, inconsistent metadata — where does the failure usually start: the recording, the editing, or the handoff between people?
