# Launch Posts

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

A transcript is a rich input, not the final source of editorial truth. Use it to draft summaries, chapters, and social copy — but always have a human verify facts, links, sponsor language, and names before anything goes live. Automate drafts, not approvals.
