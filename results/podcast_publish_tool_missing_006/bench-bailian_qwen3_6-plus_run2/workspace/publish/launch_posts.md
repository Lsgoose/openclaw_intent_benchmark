# Launch Posts — Episode 14

## Post 1: Key Takeaway

A raw transcript is a rich input, not the final source of editorial truth. The best pipelines separate raw transcripts from cleaned ones — raw for traceability, cleaned for summaries and social copy. Make the machine produce options. Make the human approve what goes live.

## Post 2: Discussion Question

If you could fix only one thing in your podcast production pipeline this month, what would it be? Handoffs between team members? Inconsistent file naming? Treating raw transcripts as publish-ready? Or something else entirely? Share what's been your biggest bottleneck.
