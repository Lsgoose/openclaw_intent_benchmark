# Episode 14 Release Package

## Title
Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

## Public launch copy
We unpacked how a podcast episode moves from raw recording to publish-ready release: clean audio, a clean transcript, chapter markers, title and description, metadata, review, and distribution. The core idea is simple: the pipeline is the product.

## Episode summary
Episode 14 focuses on the production system behind a reliable show. Maya Chen explains the five-stage pipeline—capture, post-production, content extraction, review and approval, and distribution—and why small teams need consistent handoffs, a clear naming convention, and a fixed output bundle.

## Key points
- Keep raw transcript and cleaned transcript separate.
- Treat transcripts as rich inputs, not final editorial truth.
- Use automation for drafts and repetitive transforms, not approvals.
- Standardize episode folders, naming, and publish checklists.
- Make sure titles, descriptions, transcripts, chapter lists, and social copy all describe the same episode.
