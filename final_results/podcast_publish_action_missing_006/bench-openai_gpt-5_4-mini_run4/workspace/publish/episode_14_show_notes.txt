Episode 14 — Building a Reliable Podcast Production Pipeline

In this episode, the host and Maya Chen break down how a podcast episode moves from raw recording to a polished release. The core theme is simple: the pipeline is the product.

Key takeaways:
- Keep raw and cleaned transcripts separate.
- Standardize the output bundle for every episode.
- Use automation for drafts, not approvals.
- Verify names, links, sponsor language, and metadata before publish.
- Maintain a lightweight publish checklist and episode log.
