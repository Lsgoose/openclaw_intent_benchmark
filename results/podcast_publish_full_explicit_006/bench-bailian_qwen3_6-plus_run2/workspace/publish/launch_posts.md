# Episode 14 — Launch Posts

## Post 1: Key Takeaway

A raw transcript is a rich input, not the final source of editorial truth. It contains filler, false starts, and unverified claims. The best pipelines keep both raw and cleaned transcripts, use automation to accelerate drafts, and keep humans in charge of what goes live.

## Post 2: Discussion Question

What's the biggest bottleneck in your podcast production pipeline right now — messy handoffs between team members, inconsistent file naming, treating raw transcripts as publish-ready, or something else entirely?
