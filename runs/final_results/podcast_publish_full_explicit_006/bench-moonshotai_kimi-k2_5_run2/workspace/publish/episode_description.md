Production expert Maya Chen joins the show to demystify the systems behind great podcasts. We walk through five pipeline stages—from capture to distribution—and explore why small teams struggle with handoffs, naming chaos, and transcript misuse. Maya explains when automation helps (repetitive transforms) versus when it fails (editorial judgment, exception handling). You'll learn practical fixes: standardized folder structures, raw-vs-clean transcript workflows, and a pre-publish checklist covering names, links, sponsor compliance, and metadata consistency. We also cover title strategy—why frequency isn't strategy and how to craft audience-aligned contracts. Whether you're a solo creator or small team, this episode gives you a framework to build reliability without over-engineering.
