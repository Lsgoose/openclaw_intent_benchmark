# Launch Posts — Episode 14

**Post 1 — Key Takeaway:**
A podcast transcript is a rich input, not the final source of editorial truth. Raw transcripts contain filler, corrections, and unverified claims — clean and review before publishing anything derivative. Automate drafts, not approvals.

**Post 2 — Discussion Question:**
What's the first thing that breaks in your podcast workflow when things get busy — handoffs, naming conventions, or something else entirely? Getting specific about failure modes is how you build a pipeline that actually holds up.
