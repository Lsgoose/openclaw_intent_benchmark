Episode 14 looks at the podcast production pipeline as the real product behind a polished show. Maya Chen and the host break down the five stages: capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution. They explore why raw transcripts are useful but not publish-ready, how cleaned transcripts support summaries and clips, and where small teams usually lose time through handoffs, naming chaos, and missing checks. The episode also covers title strategy, metadata consistency, sponsor review, and a simple rule for automation: use machines to draft, not to approve. A practical closing section offers a lightweight system any small team can adopt to make releases more reliable.
