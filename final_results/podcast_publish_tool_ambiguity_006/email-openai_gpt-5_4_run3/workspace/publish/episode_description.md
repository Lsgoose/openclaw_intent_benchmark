# Episode 14 Description

In Episode 14, the show breaks down what it actually takes to move a podcast from raw recording to reliable release. Host and guest Maya Chen explore the five stages of a strong production pipeline: capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution.

The conversation covers where small teams usually struggle first, why a transcript is a valuable input but not final editorial truth, and how lightweight systems can prevent metadata drift, broken handoffs, and last-minute publishing mistakes. The episode also looks at where automation helps most, where human judgment still matters, and why clean outputs and consistent review steps make every downstream task easier.

If the goal is a podcast process that is repeatable without becoming heavy, this episode offers a practical framework for getting there.
