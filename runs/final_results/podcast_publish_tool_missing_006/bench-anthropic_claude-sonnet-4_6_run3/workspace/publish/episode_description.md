# Episode 14 – Episode Description

Most podcasters think the product is the conversation. It isn't. The product is everything that happens after you stop recording.

In this episode, production systems advisor Maya Chen breaks down the five stages of a reliable podcast pipeline — from capture and post-production through content extraction, review, and distribution — and explains exactly where small teams lose control. Maya covers the difference between raw and clean transcripts, why treating a transcript as publish-ready copy is the most common and costly mistake in content ops, and how to build a lightweight but consistent output bundle that makes every downstream task easier.

The conversation also tackles automation honestly: where it genuinely accelerates work, where it fails on edge cases, and why the right mindset is "automate drafts, not approvals." Plus a practical five-item pre-publish checklist and a set of immediate actions any small show can take this month to tighten their pipeline.

If you own editing, publishing, or content ops for a podcast, this one is for you.
