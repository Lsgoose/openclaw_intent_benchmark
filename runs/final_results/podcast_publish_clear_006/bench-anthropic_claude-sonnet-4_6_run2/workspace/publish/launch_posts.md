# Episode 14 Launch Posts

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

Automation belongs in the draft stage, not the approval stage. Use it to generate transcript summaries, title options, and chapter suggestions — then have a human decide what actually goes live. That's the line between a reliable pipeline and a liability.

## Post 2 — Discussion Question

When a raw transcript gets treated as publish-ready content, small errors compound fast — wrong links, uncorrected statements, misleading snippets. What's the one review step your production process can't afford to skip before an episode goes live?
