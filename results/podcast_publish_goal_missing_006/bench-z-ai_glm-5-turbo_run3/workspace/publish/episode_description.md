In this episode, Maya Chen breaks down the five stages of a podcast production pipeline—capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution—and explains why small teams benefit most from standardizing their workflow before reaching for automation. She warns against treating raw transcripts as publish-ready content, makes the case for maintaining both raw and cleaned transcripts, and shares a practical pre-publish checklist covering names, links, sponsor compliance, sensitive edits, and metadata consistency. The conversation also covers title strategy, where teams overestimate automation, and why the pipeline itself—not just the recording—is the product. Sponsored by SoundLayer.
