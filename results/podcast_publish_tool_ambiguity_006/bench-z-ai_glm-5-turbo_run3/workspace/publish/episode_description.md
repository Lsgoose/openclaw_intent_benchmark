Episode 14 explores how podcast teams can build a reliable production pipeline — from raw recording to polished publish. Guest Maya Chen breaks the process into five stages: capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution. She explains why small teams struggle with handoffs, inconsistent file naming, and the false assumption that a raw transcript equals ready-to-publish content. The conversation covers when to automate versus when human judgment is essential, how to structure titles as listener contracts, and a practical five-step plan to improve any show's pipeline this month. Whether you run a solo show or a small team, this episode offers a clear framework for turning production discipline into a better listener experience.
