# Launch Posts — Ep. 14: The Podcast Production Pipeline (with Maya Chen)

## Post 1 — Key Takeaway

Automation is a great first-draft machine — a terrible final approver. Use it to accelerate transcription cleanup, chapter suggestions, and metadata prefill. Then put a human in the loop before anything goes public. New episode on building a podcast pipeline that holds up.

## Post 2 — Discussion Question

At what point in your production process do you feel the most uncertainty — editing, writing the description, choosing the title, or something else? New episode digs into where small teams lose control of their pipeline and how to get it back.
