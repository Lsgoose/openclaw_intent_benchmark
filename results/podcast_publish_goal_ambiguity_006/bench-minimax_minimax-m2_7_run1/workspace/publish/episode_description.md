# Episode Description

Every podcast episode starts as a raw conversation and ends as a polished product—but the gap between those two points is where production quality either earns trust or loses it. In this episode, Maya Chen breaks down the five-stage production pipeline: capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution. She explains why small teams hit friction first on handoffs and file naming, how raw transcripts differ from clean ones (and why that distinction matters for downstream content), and where automation actually helps versus where human judgment stays irreplaceable. Maya also shares her framework for generating strong title candidates, the five quality checks every team should run before publishing, and a practical starting point for shows that want to tighten up their process this month. A must-listen for media teams, creator businesses, and anyone experimenting with AI-assisted production.
