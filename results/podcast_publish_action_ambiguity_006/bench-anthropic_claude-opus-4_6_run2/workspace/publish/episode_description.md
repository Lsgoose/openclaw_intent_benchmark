# Episode 14 — Description

## Short Description

Maya Chen explains the five stages of a reliable podcast production pipeline and shares practical steps small teams can take to standardize their process this month.

## Long Description

How does a raw recording become a polished episode with clean audio, accurate show notes, chapter markers, and social clips? In Episode 14, production systems advisor Maya Chen walks through the five stages every podcast pipeline needs — capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution — and explains where small teams most often stumble. The conversation covers why separating raw and cleaned transcripts prevents downstream quality problems, where automation genuinely helps versus where editorial judgment is irreplaceable, and a concrete checklist for improving your workflow without building an elaborate system. Whether you produce a show solo or with a small team, this episode offers a clear framework for making your production process more consistent and less fragile.
