# Episode 14 Description

Episode 14 explores what it takes to build a reliable podcast production pipeline. Host and guest Maya Chen walk through the five stages of production: capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution. The conversation focuses on where small teams usually break first, why transcripts are valuable but not a final source of truth, and how a simple, repeatable output bundle can improve quality across every release surface.

The episode also covers where automation helps most, where human judgment still matters, how to generate stronger title options, and which pre-publish checks catch the highest-cost mistakes. The practical takeaway is straightforward: standardize folders, naming, transcripts, review steps, and final assets before trying to automate everything.
