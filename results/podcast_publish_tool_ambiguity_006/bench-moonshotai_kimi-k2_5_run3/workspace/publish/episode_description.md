Maya Chen joins the show to unpack how podcast production pipelines shape listener experience — and why small teams struggle most at the handoffs. We walk through five stages from capture to distribution, explain why transcripts need both raw and cleaned versions, and explore where automation genuinely helps versus where it risks producing misleading content. You'll hear practical advice on pre-publish review checklists, title strategy, and why a title is a contract with the listener, not just a label. Whether you're a team of two or twenty, this episode offers a concrete framework for building consistency without drowning in process.
