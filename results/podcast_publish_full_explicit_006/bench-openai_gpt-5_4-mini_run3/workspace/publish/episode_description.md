Episode 14 breaks down how to build a reliable podcast production pipeline from raw recording to release. Maya Chen explains the five core stages: capture, post-production, content extraction, review, and distribution. The conversation covers why clean transcripts matter, how to separate raw and cleaned source material, where automation helps, where human judgment still matters, and which pre-publish checks prevent avoidable mistakes. It also offers a practical small-team workflow: standard folders, consistent naming, a defined output bundle, and a checklist that keeps titles, descriptions, chapters, and social copy aligned. A concise guide for anyone trying to make podcast publishing less chaotic and more repeatable.
